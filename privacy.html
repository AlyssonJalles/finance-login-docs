Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how finance-login ("the application", "we"), operated by Alysson Jalles, accesses, uses, and protects your information when you sign in with Google.

1. Information we access

When you sign in with Google, the application accesses your basic profile information: name, email address, and profile picture. In addition, with your explicit consent, the application requests the Gmail scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.modify, which allows it to read, organize (for example, label or archive), and send email messages on your behalf. This access is used exclusively to process personal-finance-related email messages (such as transaction notifications) as part of the features you invoke. The application never reads, modifies, or sends email for any purpose other than the functionality you explicitly request.

2. How we use your information

The information listed above is used solely to operate the application's features. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties except as required to provide the service or comply with the law. No Google user data is used for advertising, and no humans read your data except with your explicit permission for support or security purposes.

3. Data storage and retention

All data processed by the application, including OAuth tokens and any email-derived content, is stored locally on your own device. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your Google user data. Data remains on your device until you delete it, and the application's access can be revoked at any time (see Section 5).

4. Google API Services — Limited Use disclosure

finance-login's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Revoking access

You may revoke the application's access to your Google account at any time via the Google Account permissions page. You may also request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us at the email below.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), to protect your information during transmission and processing.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

For any questions about this policy or your data, contact jalles10@gmail.com.
