A secure Google sign-in integration for accessing your personal finance dashboard.

About this application

mobills-login uses Google OAuth to provide secure sign-in. It connects your Google account to a private, personal finance dashboard, processing finance-related email messages on your behalf so your transaction data stays organized and up to date.
