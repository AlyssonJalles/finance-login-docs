finance-login

A personal finance automation app that keeps your private financial dashboard up to date using your Google account.

What is finance-login?

finance-login is a personal productivity application developed by Alysson Jalles. Its purpose is to automate the collection and organization of an individual user's own financial information — such as transaction alerts and statements received by email — into a private, personal finance dashboard, so the user always has an up-to-date view of their finances without manual data entry.

What the app does

  • Secure sign-in with Google — you authenticate with your own Google account through the official Google OAuth flow; the app never sees your password.
  • Reads finance-related email — with your consent, it scans your Gmail inbox for messages such as bank transaction notifications and statements.
  • Organizes your data — extracted transaction data is categorized and consolidated into your private dashboard.
  • Keeps everything on your device — all processing and storage happen locally; no data is sent to third-party servers.

Why does the app request Gmail access?

The app requests the Gmail permission (gmail.modify) solely to read finance-related messages and mark or label them as processed, so the same transaction is not imported twice. It does not read unrelated mail content for any other purpose, and it never sends email on your behalf without an action you explicitly initiate.
